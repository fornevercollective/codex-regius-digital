## Organ of the Language
- **Phonology**: Old West Norse (Icelandic transmission)
- **Meter**: Fornyrðislag / ljóðaháttr (poem-dependent)
- **Oral layer**: Eddic poems preserve pre-Christian mythological strata with Christian framing in places
- **Carving trace**: Younger Futhark inscriptions and runestone formulas as distant cognates, not direct liturgical copies

## Thematic Cross-References (Christian ↔ Pagan parallels)
- **Cosmogony & world-tree**: Yggdrasil, Ginnungagap, Ragnarök cycle ↔ Genesis creation narrative, Apocalyptic revelation literature
- **Heroic death & afterlife**: Valhǫll, Hel, fylgja ↔ Judgment, soul's destination
- **Wisdom contest / flyting**: Óðinn as wisdom-seeker ↔ Solomon wisdom literature (structural)
